## 1. Build a Personal AI Brand & Secure Sponsorships
Build a personal brand by filming yourself sharing useful AI tips and tricks on social media. No specialized technical knowledge is required; content can be derived from documentation or by asking AI tools for scripts. Focus on short-form platforms like TikTok for explosive growth, then cross-post to Instagram, YouTube Shorts, Facebook, and Threads. AI startups are actively seeking creators for sponsorships and collaborations, offering significant payment for promoting their products. Learn by copying viral content from established creators and gradually develop your unique style.

---

## 2. Create Profitable Faceless Videos
Explore three avenues for making money with faceless videos using AI:
*   **TikTok Shop Videos:** Generate videos featuring AI avatars interacting with physical products for e-commerce.
*   **AI Avatar Content:** Create an AI avatar (e.g., a motivational monk) to produce content on topics like wellness or mindset, monetizing through sales of simple products linked in the bio.
*   **AI Avatar Services for Businesses:** Offer to create high-quality AI clones of business owners (including custom voice) to generate social media content, saving them time in filming and editing. A key piece of advice: always validate content manually before attempting to automate production.

---

## 3. Offer Claude Training for Businesses
Capitalize on the growing adoption of Claude by businesses seeking to enhance productivity. Position yourself as an expert in Claude training, covering topics such as prompting, skill building, team collaboration, project setup, and task automation within Claude. The value proposition is clear: save businesses time and money (e.g., 5 hours per week per person, charging $5k-$10k for a one-day training). Acquire clients by combining content creation with targeted cold outreach on platforms like Instagram and LinkedIn, leading with value and offering lead magnets.

---

## 4. "Vibe Code" Simple AI Apps & Prioritize Marketing
Avoid two common mistakes when developing AI apps: overcomplicating the first version and neglecting marketing. Start with a single, simple feature that provides immediate value to users (e.g., a calorie tracker from a photo). Simplicity aids in description, word-of-mouth sharing, and content creation by influencers. Post-launch, dedicate 90% of your efforts to marketing. Leverage resources like socialgrowthengineers.com for consumer marketing trends. Market your app by creating your own social media content (applying viral formats) or by paying micro-influencers who consistently achieve high view counts. The ultimate goal is to discover and repeatedly use a "repeatable viral format."

---

## 5. Implement AI Marketing Automations for Businesses
Offer specialized AI marketing automation services to business owners and creators to help them convert attention into revenue more efficiently. Focus on three distinct automations:
*   **Go High Level Setup with AI:** Configure this powerful, white-label CRM, integrating AI to intelligently respond to inbound leads, qualify customers, and book meetings based on the company's knowledge base.
*   **ManyChat AI Automations (Facebook/Instagram):** Identify fast-growing creators or businesses with inefficient lead follow-up processes on social media. Offer to streamline their pipeline using ManyChat AI to convert social media engagement into sales.
*   **LinkedIn Automation Tools (e.g., Expandi, PhantomBuster, Lead Shark):** Help founders and business owners safely automate LinkedIn outreach and engagement to convert their professional network traffic into revenue, a high-ROI use case.

---

## Conclusion
These five methods—building a personal AI brand, creating faceless videos, offering Claude training, developing simple AI apps with strong marketing, and implementing AI marketing automations—provide viable pathways to make money with AI in 2026, even for beginners. None of these require extensive prior technical skill; learning occurs through the process. The most critical factor for success is choosing a path that personally resonates and sticking with it consistently to realize compounding returns.
