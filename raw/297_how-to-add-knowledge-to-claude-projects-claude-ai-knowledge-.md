## 1. Navigating and Creating Projects
▶ [0:09–0:31](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gG8CiwRy2jc&t=9s)
This section guides users on how to access their projects tab, located on the left side of the screen. It explains that existing projects will be visible, and for new users, instructions are provided to create a new project by clicking "new project" and assigning a title and optional description.

---

## 2. Understanding Cloud Project Components
▶ [0:31–0:39](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gG8CiwRy2jc&t=31s)
Once inside a selected project, users will see three key components: project memory, instructions, and files (also referred to as the knowledge base). These elements collectively define how the AI will interact and generate responses within that specific project.

---

## 3. Adding Files to Your Knowledge Base
▶ [0:39–0:57](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gG8CiwRy2jc&t=39s)
To enrich your project's knowledge, files can be easily added to the knowledge base. This is done by clicking the plus icon and selecting "upload from device" or other relevant upload options. The uploaded files will then become part of the project's accessible knowledge.

---

## 4. Impact of Knowledge Base on AI Responses
▶ [0:57–1:23](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gG8CiwRy2jc&t=57s)
After a file is uploaded, any subsequent AI chat within that project will begin referencing the information contained in the new file. This means the AI will use the document's content (e.g., scientific journals, specific data) to generate more informed and contextually relevant responses, effectively leveraging the added knowledge.

---

## 5. Achieving Consistent Output with Instructions
▶ [1:23–2:08](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gG8CiwRy2jc&t=83s)
The video highlights the utility of combining the knowledge base with project-specific instructions. By doing so, users can consistently achieve similar types or styles of responses across different chats. This ensures uniformity and predictability in the AI's output, aligning it with predefined guidelines and loaded knowledge.

---

## Conclusion
▶ [2:08–2:21](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gG8CiwRy2jc&t=128s)
Adding a knowledge base to your cloud projects is a powerful way to tailor AI interactions. By providing specific documents and instructions, you can guide the AI to produce consistent, relevant, and context-aware responses, significantly enhancing the utility of your projects.
