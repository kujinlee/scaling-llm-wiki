The Power of Self-Scaffolding (3:19–4:23) dig deeper ▶

Ornith's self-scaffolding process allows it to learn "how to work" efficiently over thousands of rounds, outperforming larger competitors like Quinn 3.5.

  • Ornith learns to design, refine, and rewrite its own operational process, unlike models with fixed human-written scaffolds.
