## 1. Build an AI Personal Brand for Sponsorships
▶ [1:01–5:56](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xl57Pki6ChE&t=61s)
Create short-form AI educational content by rephrasing documentation or using AI tools to generate scripts. Platforms like TikTok offer explosive growth for new accounts, which can then be cross-posted. As your following grows, AI startups, flush with cash and seeking attention, will offer sponsorships and collaborations, providing a significant monetization stream. No prior technical expertise is needed; consistency in content creation is key.

---

## 2. Create Faceless AI Videos
▶ [5:56–9:45](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xl57Pki6ChE&t=356s)
Explore three validated ideas for faceless videos: generating TikTok Shop product promotion videos with AI avatars, creating viral motivational content using AI monk avatars (selling simple products in bio), or offering AI avatar creation services to busy business owners. The critical advice is to avoid premature automation; first, find what works manually, then selectively introduce automation.

---

## 3. Offer Claude Training for Businesses
▶ [9:45–12:45](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xl57Pki6ChE&t=585s)
Capitalize on the growing migration to and popularity of Claude, especially for its Co-work and Code features. Position yourself to train business teams on effective Claude usage for prompting, skill building, team collaboration, and project management. The pitch should focus on saving businesses time (e.g., 5 hours/week per person) and increasing productivity, justifying a significant fee. Acquire clients through cold outreach and lead magnets on platforms like Instagram and LinkedIn.

---

## 4. Vibe Coding Single-Feature AI Apps
▶ [12:45–21:40](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xl57Pki6ChE&t=765s)
Avoid two common mistakes: overcomplicating the first version and neglecting marketing post-launch. Develop AI apps with a single, clear feature that provides immediate value (e.g., Calorie AI). After launching, dedicate at least 90% of your effort to marketing. Utilize resources like `socialgrowthengineers.com` for viral marketing insights and leverage micro-influencers or self-promotion to discover and repeat successful viral content formats.

---

## 5. Set Up AI Marketing Automations
▶ [21:40–26:28](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Xl57Pki6ChE&t=1300s)
Offer services to businesses for setting up specific AI marketing automations. This includes configuring Go High Level (a white-label CRM) with AI-powered chat and email workflows, implementing ManyChat AI automations for Instagram and Facebook to fix broken lead conversion funnels, and setting up LinkedIn automation tools (like Expandi or PhantomBuster) to convert traffic into revenue. These high-ROI services directly address business owners' needs to convert attention into sales.
