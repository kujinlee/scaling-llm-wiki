## 2. The Fluidity of Research and Applied Roles
▶ [3:03–13:11](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cDyi91onoJ8&t=183s)
While a distinction between applied and research organizations exists, the lines are increasingly blurred. Even in product-specific verticals, significant research is required, such as ensuring LLM factual accuracy and source citation for search results. Both pure research (e.g., pre-training, post-training) and applied roles demand individuals to be fluid across the entire research-to-applied spectrum, often requiring responsibility for both model development and stable training operations.
